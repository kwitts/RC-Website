Referralclix Features

HOW DO MARKETERS CREATE, MANAGE, AND TRACK THE REFERRAL PROGRAM?

Referral Program Configuration

Easily control all aspect of the referral program such as requirements for the referrer to earn a reward, different social, or email types of referrals offered, rewards, discounts for referred friends, redemption criteria, coupon expiration, and many more rules designed for optimal conversion.

Referral Parameters

Set it and forget it. With our easy to use campaign creation tool easily trigger customer refer-a-friend modal popups based on SKU, min/max cart value, new customer identification or existing customer.

Inviting

Target a customer list based on certain criteria or upload a custom list to participate in your referral program or introduce a new product.

Coupons

Create a coupon in Demandware and easily attach it to the referral program with our seamless integration or simply create a coupon in ReferralClix.

Reporting

Get an easy-to-use dashboard incorporating analytics and charts that present key program performance data.

Customer Visibility

Access customer profile and history, as well as administer reward capabilities.

Social Integrations

Integrate with Facebook, Twitter, and other social platforms.

Transactional Email

Trigger emails based on activity such as invite, reward earned, offer sent, reminder, redemption, campaign end, suspension, and termination.
