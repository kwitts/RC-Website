referral process as a service

it's not just about the technology

To create a successful referral program at the enterprise level, it takes more than just software. We have deep knowledge of referral program best practices based on successful deployments across clients with sophisticated business models and omni channel marketing strategies.

We focus on 8 defining foundational aspects to deliver results:

  • Existing business model and acquisition strategies
    • Assign referral team leader to thoroughly discuss program goals, objectives and desired outcome.
    • We blueprint and visually document existing customer acquisition channels.
    • Create an action plan and scope of work with all requirements.
  • Referral program design
    • Make available to all users
    • Promotion
    • Program placement
  • Clear Call to Action
    • What’s in it for sharing with friends
    • Friend offer must be easily redeemed
    • Reinforce the offer when the friend clicks and lands on website
  • Compelling Content
    • Treat every sentence like a paragraph
    • Less is more
    • Optimize the call to action (A/B testing)
  • Meaningful Rewards
    • How and when to earn a reward
    • Match reward to buyer preference
    • Meaningful reward to friends
  • Sharing Types
    • Facebook, Twitter, Google+
    • Email
    • Mass Uploader
  • Referral Program Entry Points
    • Landing Page
    • Pre
    • Post Purchase Overlays
    • Blogs
    • All Emails
    • Dedicated Page on Site
    • Ribbons
    • Member Account
